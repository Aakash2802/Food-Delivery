## Quick Login Guide

### For Customers:
Use `customer@demo.com` / `password123` to browse and order food

### For Restaurant Vendors:
Login with your restaurant-specific email to:
- Manage menu items
- View and accept orders
- Update restaurant status
- Track sales

### For Drivers:
Use `driver@demo.com` / `password123` to:
- View assigned deliveries
- Update delivery status
- Track locations

### For Admin:
Use `admin@demo.com` / `password123` to:
- Manage all users
- View all restaurants
- Monitor all orders
- System administration
